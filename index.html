About Me

As a child, my life revolved around moving from place to place, which really felt like an adventure. I lived in five different countries, growing up surrounded by fascinating cultures and people. This upbringing instilled in me a deep curiosity about the world and a strong ability to connect with those around me. It also created an inner desire to understand how things work on a fundamental level, and how I could use similarities to bridge differences. I have always been fascinated by technology, from newly released iPhones to desktop computers, and I spent much of my time learning about these technological developments. Curiosity has always been my engine. I believe the future is the present. Thus, I was fascinated by the notion of machine learning and the idea that a computer could learn, feel and even connect like a real person.

I would also like to shatter your prejudice. Yes, I do love technology and computers, but I am also a great fan of sports, both playing and following. My favorite sport is basketball, which has always been a passion of mine. I enjoy working out, traveling the world, hiking, cooking, and music. I also love languages and learning about other cultures, as I am fluent in both English and Hebrew, and I am currently learning Spanish. I hope to pursue a path that allows me to continue seeing the world and its diverse cultures, while making a positive impact wherever I am.
